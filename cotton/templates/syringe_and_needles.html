Syringes and Needles

Available Syringe Capacities: 1 ml | 2 ml | 5 ml | 10 ml | 20 ml

Packaging:100 pcs boxes (disposable syringes)

Our Syringes and Needles are precision-engineered for safe, hygienic, and efficient administration of medications, vaccines, and other injectable solutions. Designed for medical, laboratory, and clinical use, they provide accurate dosage, smooth injection, and minimal discomfort for patients.

Features

  • Sterile and single-use for maximum safety
  • High-quality, medical-grade plastic and stainless steel
  • Available in multiple sizes and gauges
  • Smooth, precise injection for minimal pain
  • Transparent barrel for accurate measurement
  • Needle options: fixed, detachable, or retractable
  • Compliant with international medical standards

Uses

  • Administering vaccines and medications
  • Drawing blood or biological samples
  • Laboratory and diagnostic purposes
  • Insulin delivery for diabetic patients
  • Clinical and hospital procedures
  • Emergency medical kits
